How it works

From public filings to your comparison — transparently

  1. 1

    Regulators publish the rules

    US carriers must file their rates, rules, and rating formulas with state regulators before charging them. Those filings are public record.

  2. 2

    AI reads every filing

    Our AI pipeline extracts each carrier’s rating plan — base rates, factor tables, the exact formula — and verifies it against the carrier’s own worked examples in the filing.

  3. 3

    Prices computed for your situation

    Describe your situation once; the hub computes representative prices across carriers, side by side, at national scale.

  4. 4

    Refreshed when rates change

    A new filing is literally the event “this carrier’s prices changed.” We watch for them and refresh — often before the change even takes effect.

Prices are representative rates computed from public filings — carriers confirm the final quote. What you get here is the honest ranking, with the receipts.
